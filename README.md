# 🚀 NexTrade: Institutional Prediction Market

**NexTrade** brings a professional, centralized exchange (CEX) experience to decentralized prediction markets on the Shardeum network. 

## ⚠️ The Problem
Current prediction markets feel like clunky, casual betting sites. They use overly simplistic "vote" buttons and lack the advanced tools—like deep order books, live charting, and limit orders—that serious traders need to manage risk and provide deep liquidity. Furthermore, users are forced to jump between different apps to manage their Web3 wallets and portfolios.

## 💡 The Solution
We are the first platform to combine a Binance-style, dark-mode trading interface with a trustless, blockchain-based outcome resolution system. Instead of just "voting", users trade "YES" and "NO" outcome shares on a high-frequency trading terminal, with funds securely locked and resolved via Shardeum smart contracts.

## ✨ Key Features
* **Pro-Grade Trade Terminal:** High-performance interface featuring simulated live candlestick charting and real-time order books.
* **Smart Contract Integration:** Trustless fund locking and outcome resolution built natively on Shardeum.
* **Unified Dashboard:** A real-time, holistic view of asset portfolios, market movers, and recent transactions.
* **Frictionless On-Ramping:** Integrated wallet flows with auto-generated network addresses and Web3 connection.

## 🛠 Tech Stack
* **Frontend:** Next.js, Tailwind CSS, TypeScript, Ethers.js
* **Blockchain:** Solidity, Shardeum Sphinx Testnet
* **UI/UX:** Custom Glassmorphism & High-Density Pro Trading Interface
